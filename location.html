The Garment Factory is located in the heart of the vibrant Merchant City and only a 2 minute walk from Merchant Square, a well known food, drink and nightlife destination.

Merchant City

Merchant City is just minutes away from George Square and the Style Mile shopping district.It is one of Glasgow’s most vibrant areas brimming with art, culture, and fashion and complete with fabulous places to eat and drink. It hosts a number of nationally recognised restaurant names alongside eclectic independent bars, galleries and quirky little shops to explore.

Also just moments away sits Merchant Square – a stylish hub of independent bars and restaurants all under one roof.
